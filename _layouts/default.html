About Me

Hi there!

My name is Jhonatas Rodrigues Machado. I hold a Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science. I am currently working full-time as a Software Developer on the Oracle Commerce Cloud platform at Compass.UOL, where I lead projects with clients that have a national and global reach in their respective markets. Some of the projects that I had the opportunity to participate in are:

  • Brasif Máquinas
  • Grendene
  • Osklen Brazil
  • Osklen USA
  • Rider
  • Salon Line
  • Yamaha Motor Brazil
  • Yamaha Nautical
  • Yamaha Riding Academy

Before starting to work at Compass.UOL, I was a freelance developer on web and mobile technologies, as well as a developer of Add-ons for the Firefox browser. I also had the opportunity to develop in several startups and large open-source corporations on products such as Firefox for iOS and Firefox Lockwise.

For the next years, I want to be a great and essential team player at the company where I work, always looking for the growth of the company and myself. In the future, I want to have the opportunity to lead an innovative team while seeking an extraordinary approach to every single project. As a part of a team, I am always open to challenges and constructive feedback from my teammates and clients.

Since 2014, I have been a Core Contributor at Mozilla. When possible, I strengthen the community by developing tools to help volunteers and staff in their contributions, like the Add-on SUMO Live Helper. During all these years, I have been contributing to Mozilla with functions of leadership, localization, administration of the community website, support, and development (Add-ons for Firefox and apps for Android and iOS). Over these years, I had the opportunity to meet collaborators from different countries at various events, in places like Hawaii, San Francisco, Orlando, Berlin, Vancouver, and Whistler.


Thank you for taking the time to get to know me.
