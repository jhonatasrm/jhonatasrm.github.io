About Me

Hello! My name is Jhonatas Rodrigues. I hold a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science. I am currently looking for a full-time position in the software industry. I have been working as a freelance developer on web and mobile technologies for the last five years, and I’ve interacted with several startups and large open-source corporations like Mozilla on Firefox for iOS and Firefox Lockwise. My plans for the next five years include working with an organization that prides itself on caring for employees, users, digital privacy, and security. In the future, I would like to lead a team that embraces those values as well. If I am successfully hired to be part of your team, I can provide an open-minded, fresh take, and an extraordinary approach to every project. Additionally, I am always open to challenges and constructive feedback. After working with multiple organizations, my teammates recognize me as a fast learner and reliable team player, where I dedicate my time and experience to develop high-quality and unique products.


I am presently a Community Team Leader at Mozilla Brazil and have been a core contributor since 2014. I continue to strengthen Mozilla’s community by developing tools to improve volunteer and staff contributions. Moreover, I am a translator, editor, and reviewer of knowledge base articles, and I provide social media support through official Firefox channels (Twitter and Google Play).


Thank you for taking the time to get to know me better. If you believe I will be a great addition to your organization, please feel free to reach out to me.
