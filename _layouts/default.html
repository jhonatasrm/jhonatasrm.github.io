About Me

Hi there!

My name is Jhonatas Rodrigues. I hold a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science. I am currently working full-time as a Front-End Developer at Compasso, where I'm very proud to be part of a fantastic company. Before starting to work at Compasso, I was a freelance developer on web and mobile technologies, developing extensions for the Firefox browser. I also had the opportunity to interact with several startups and large open-source corporations on products such as Firefox for iOS and Firefox Lockwise.

For the next years, I want to be a great and essential team player at Compasso, always looking for the growth of the company and me as well. In the future, I want to have the opportunity to lead an innovative team while seeking an extraordinary approach to every single project. As a part of an excellent team, I am always open to challenges and constructive feedback from my teammates.


I am a Core Contributor at Mozilla since 2014 and also a Community Team Leader at Mozilla Brasil. I continue to strengthen the community by developing tools to improve volunteer and staff contributions like SUMO Live Helper. Moreover, I am a translator, editor, also a reviewer of the knowledge base articles, responsible for the Mozilla Brazil Website, and I provide social media support through official Firefox channels (Twitter and Google Play).


Thank you for taking the time to get to know me better.

- JRM
